CTO & Technical Leadership for Modern Product Teams

I'm Henrik Oscarsson, a CTO and senior engineering leader with 25 years of experience building and scaling complex software systems — primarily in finance, trading, and high-transaction payment environments. I work at the intersection of technology, business, and execution, helping companies deliver robust platforms with speed, clarity, and long-term sustainability. Over the years, I've supported organizations ranging from global enterprises to fast-moving product teams, often stepping in where technical direction, architectural clarity, and delivery focus are critical. My role is to ensure that technology becomes an enabler of growth — not a bottleneck.

Building Strong Teams Through Mentorship and Architecture.

My work often goes beyond systems design — I take an active role in mentoring engineers, strengthening team structure, and raising the technical standard across an organization. I enjoy building environments where developers can thrive, grow in confidence, and deliver high-quality software together. I've led teams in both startup and enterprise settings, and I'm comfortable in CTO-level steering groups where technology decisions must align tightly with business goals. Clear communication between engineering and leadership is often the key to successful execution, and it's a space where I bring long experience.

AI-First Engineering.

Today, I apply an AI-first approach to software development and technical leadership — using modern tools such as Claude and AI-assisted workflows to increase delivery speed, improve developer productivity, and reduce unnecessary complexity. The goal is not automation for its own sake, but building calmer, sharper, more effective engineering teams. When AI is introduced thoughtfully, it becomes a force multiplier: helping teams stay focused, ship faster, and spend more time solving the right problems.
